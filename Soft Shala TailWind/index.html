Frequently Asked Questions

What is Share Hub Nepal?

Share Hub Nepal is a mobile application that provides tools and data for investors in the Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE), including portfolio tracking, stock updates, and analysis tools.

How can I download the app?

You can download Share Hub Nepal from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store by searching for "Share Hub Nepal".

Is the app free to use?

Yes, Share Hub Nepal is free to download and use, with optional premium features available for purchase.

What features does the app offer?

The app offers real-time stock updates, portfolio management, market news, technical analysis tools, and more.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the app or via our website for any assistance or inquiries.
