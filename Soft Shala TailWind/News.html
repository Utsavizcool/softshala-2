Share Hub App: Recent Updates

The Share Hub app, a popular NEPSE stock portfolio management tool, has recently been updated to version 10.0.6. The latest update includes features such as Odd Lot handling, Facebook login fixes, and various bug fixes. The app offers real-time NEPSE alerts, IPO/FPO notifications, advanced floorsheet analysis, and more.
